This report identifies and catalogs the prosecution's strongest evidence against the defense in United States v. Creeden. The evidence falls into several devastating categories:

The prosecution's case is built on insider witnesses combined with extensive documentary evidence. Defense must be prepared to counter each theme with mitigation strategies.
